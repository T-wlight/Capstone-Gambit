This game contains both PVE and PVP elements. Killing monsters in the game will drop fluorescent blocks,
which can be stored in designated containers after being picked up. The team that has priority 100 to Summon a boss.
For every 5, 10, 15 blocks stored, you can send a blocker that can steal the opposite fluorescent block to the enemy faction.
When the blocker is on the field, the fluorescent library will be closed. When the team stores a total of 25, 50, 75, and 100 fluorescent blocks,
they can open the invasion portal to the enemy camp to kill the player carrying the fluorescent. When the boss is summoned,
the invaded portal of the camp will be opened every 15 seconds. After the intruder kills the player, the HP will be restored to the boss of the killed camp
